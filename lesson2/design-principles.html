Visual Heirarchy

Mubasic

mubasic.com

Mubasic shows Visual Heirarchy because the headline is larger than the description of the site. It gives more emphasis on the larger text. As we look at the screenshot, we tend to read the larger text first. Which is "The #1 source for children's music."

White Space and Clean Design

Overflow

overflow.io

Overflow shows white space and clean design as you can see from the screenshot above that there are these blank spaces around the elements in the website. You will notice that the buttons and the paragraphs are separate.

Alignment

Razer Gold

gold.razer.com

Razer Gold shows alignment because the elements are aligned both vertically and horizontally. As you notice the images, they are vertically aligned. And the images to the name of the game is horizontally aligned.
